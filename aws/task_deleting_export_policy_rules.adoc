= Editing an export policy rule
:toc: macro
:hardbreaks:
:nofooter:
:icons: font
:linkattrs:
:imagesdir: ./media/


[.lead]
You can delete export policy rules that are no longer needed.

.Steps
. Click the volume name, and then select *Export policy*.
. Click *X* for the policy rule that you want to delete.
. Click *Save changes*.
